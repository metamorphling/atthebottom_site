About

This blog

This is my personal blog where I write down my thoughts on industry and code. Just in case, nothing I write here is related to my employers.

Ryazan

Born in Russia, small city of Ryazan, a few hours ride from Moscow. Spent here all my years up to moving abroad. Somehow graduated from Ryazan Radio Engineering University(Software Engineering and Information Systems Management). Somehow, as in I dropped out before writing thesis. I was already earning more than I ever need and university was getting in the way of work. Once I learned that to get a specialist visa in other country one needs to finish a university, got back and did my thesis.

Sapporo

Beautiful city of Sapporo, northern capital of Japan. Moved there in 2015 and enjoyed this place for a little bit more than 2 years. At the time factually worked at ADI, in reality worked on dispatch at Fujisoft. Working by day, exploring city by night. Did travel all over Hokkaido, good times.
At some point realized that my childhood dream of working in gamedev is still burning bright, so I studied Unity in my extra-professional time. Since there aren’t many opportunities in Sapporo, my humble self-made portfolio led me to mobile game development company in Tokyo.

Tokyo

This time an actual capital. Moved in 2018 to work at Opus(“Half-Minute Hero” guys), still living here. All I can say is that we have love and hate relationships with this city. In my professional time I released a few mobile games: slot machine and card battle roguelike, both with gacha mechanics. Made a lot of friends at Opus and I’m forever grateful for opportunities I got there.

From Software

2020 brought me some surprises(besides COVID, yeah), and I managed to get into AAA game development company. Currently working as R&D engineer at From Software.
